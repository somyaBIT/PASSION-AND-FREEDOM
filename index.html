Passion & Freedom Foundation

Where Passion Meets Purpose

We are a group of Passionate and Positive people, aiming to push the conventional lifestyle, break the monotony and explore.
A passion of ours is to help inspire others to believe in themselves, and achieve things that they never thought possible.
We are not Athletes, Olympians or Superhumans.
We are a group of friends who tore up the rule book, backed ourselves, and took on our journey called #PFF.
Since doing so, we feel anything is possible with a mix of self-belief and hard-work.

News

#PFF health initiative Pedal for fun got featured in one of the top-selling lifestyle magazine Femina.

One of the prominent media houses of Gujarat covers Project Cycle Recycle story. Under this initiative, the foundation gifts refurbished bicycles as a reward to government primary school kids.

A city-based prominent newspaper covers the story of two strong ladies of #PFF family, Mari Munzani and Sangeeta Vyas. Both aged 50 and single parent covered 100 km on a Tandem Bike making it a history of being 1st Women cyclist pair to achieve such a feat in tandem in their age group. Mary and Sangeeta continue to inspire the youths of #PFF family.
