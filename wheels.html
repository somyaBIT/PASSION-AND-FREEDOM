Messengers on Wheels! Spreading positivity on humble two wheels

Published by PFF Family on March 13, 2019

Forthwith after the first ride, the founder realised that this journey is not just about pedalling, it’s a call for some higher purpose. The purpose to bring a social change by bringing positive transformation in someone’s life. This realisation within the hearts, initiated with the weekend rides, to spread the message of good health. With days, love for cycling kept increasing, and these humble two wheels brought some level of humbleness in all the messengers. It started with having a feeling of gratitude towards the surrounding and with this feeling the tribe organised a “Thanks Giving Ride” to thank the City Policeman who spend sleepless nights during the festive season to ensure that the citizens enjoy the festivities in a safe environment. And while these messengers kept organising short rides with different themes to promote Good Health and random act of Goodness, the seed of Giving that was planted during the first ride to Thol Lake started growing and turning into a strong desire to help those who are less privileged. And with no time this strong desire turned into a Passion. A Passion of spreading goodness in the society and with this newly discovered Passion, the messengers organised a short Santa Ride with a Christmas Theme where they dressed like a Santa Clause and pedalled to a nearby Orphanage run by Missionary of Charity, with the intention of spreading smiles and happiness. They even donated a small fund that they had raised through the participation in the ride.

The journey of the group continued with the highest motivation being encouraging and inspiring people to break their monotony and live an active lifestyle. Their Cycling Expedition stories started creating a buzz on Social Media, and it started showing a positive impact in their network with near and dear ones showing a keen interest in joining the group and be a part of this Noble Act of bringing positivity in people and society at large. As the tribe grew, they initially named the group as PFC (Pedal for Cause), as the group always has a social cause associated with their cycling journey. However one more thought stuck the minds, the messengers did not prefer to be looked upon as social workers on bicycles rather be known as fun loving people who want to spread positivity on their humble two wheels. Hence it was renamed as #PFF (Pedal for Fun). And the new chapter unfolds here. Amidst the new name, the group started organising different fun rides with different social themes to create Positive Social Impact. Bicycle known as an exercising or a fun tool had turned into a message of Social Change. And this led to the beginning of one of the most significant and most impactful events “WOW”.
