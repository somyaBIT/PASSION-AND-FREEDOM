Women On Wheels: Riding the wheels of Social Change

There is a strong history behind the role of bicycle in advancing women’s freedom, mobility, and position in society.
#PFF repeated this history by organizing a WOW (Women On Wheels) ride which they consider as one of the most significant events of their journey.It holds a unique position for two reasons. First, the group never imagined the level of impact a simple activity like cycling can generate to bring a Social Change.
Second, this event inspired the founder to evolve this cycling group Pedal for Fun into a Passion Platform which eventually grew as an NGO, Passion and Freedom Foundation.
The founder says, “Running an NGO was my childhood dream, but I never imagined that the path to this dream will be realized through our small Cycling initiative.”

6 AM, 7 friends, 70 km Cycling, One Story

This is an inspirational story of how a group of friends planned to make their customary weekend into an adventurous one by executing their first cycling expedition which led to the foundation of their greatest inspiration. The founding member of #PFF, Anil Nair got a bicycle as a surprise gift from his beloved wife, Sonal Nair. His close friends Kumar, Avinash and Bhavin took advantage of the opportunity and came out with an impromptu plan. “Let’s bicycle to the nearest place, amidst the lap of nature, we have already experienced the thrill of adventure in our motorbikes earlier,” said one of the friend. “70 KM, It’s damn difficult.” grumbled another. “We have become humanoids. We have lost enthusiasm in our lives. Guys, let’s relive, rejoice & have sheer fun”, reclaims Anil.
